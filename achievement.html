What are the achievements of Ibrahim El-Feki?

  • Al-Fiqi has several books, some of which have been translated into English, French, Arabic, Kurdish and Indonesian, and he has several books available in the Library and the National Archives of Canada. They consist of ten titles, all of which were published during the nineties of the last century. Their topics ranged chronologically from dealing with sales techniques through the art of successful management to writing about Self-development and success in general, among his most prominent books:

  • 1-The power of thinking

  • 2-neural linguistic programming and the art of unlimited communication

  • 3-the ten keys to success

  • 4-The power of self-control

  • 5-The power of love and forgiveness

  • 6-A life without stress

  • 7-time management

  • 8-Take control of your life

  • 9-Awaken your potential and create your future

  • 10-Driving magic
